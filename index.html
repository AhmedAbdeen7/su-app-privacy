Privacy Policy for SU App

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the AUC Student Union ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the SU App ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name: Full name from your Google account
  • Email: Email used for account identification
  • Google ID: Unique identifier for authentication
  • Phone Number: For verification and locker bookings
  • Student ID: Optional student identification number
  • Major: Optional academic major

User Content

  • Ratings, reviews, and comments on professors and courses
  • User profile information you choose to provide

Usage Data

  • Locker bookings and locations
  • Points and rewards participation

Location Data (Approximate)

  • Used only for map features to show nearby SU Card vendors
  • Location data remains on your device and is not sent to servers
  • Can be disabled in device settings

How We Use Your Information

  1. Provide and maintain app features and authentication
  2. Process locker bookings, ratings, and other requests
  3. Improve app performance and functionality
  4. Communicate account-related information
  5. Ensure security and prevent unauthorized access

Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely on protected servers
  • Industry-standard security measures are implemented
  • Authentication handled securely via Google Sign-In
  • Password is never stored by us

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In: Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps for vendor locations: Google Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do not share personal information with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics companies. Only AUC Student Union administrators access data for legitimate app purposes.

Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Disable location tracking in device settings

Contact: sutech@aucegypt.edu

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for AUC students and faculty. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Updates will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.
